# CEO Plan

## Mission
Build income outside the full-time job without blowing up time, attention, or cash flow.

## Core structure
- **Primary stability:** full-time job
- **High-margin side income:** Northory LLC
- **Scalable asset:** TopGiftsToday (affiliate/media)
- **Audience/distribution:** WireRiot
- **Experimental entertainment:** RapperVSai / music only after the core is moving

## Current priorities

### 1. Northory
Goal: land 1 high-value side client with minimal time overhead.

Focus offers:
- data pipeline audit
- analytics/data stack cleanup
- AI workflow/reporting automation
- fractional data engineering for small teams

### 2. TopGiftsToday
Goal: build a compounding affiliate content asset.

Focus:
- gift guides with buying intent
- SEO + Pinterest + short-form social
- email capture
- affiliate monetization

### 3. WireRiot
Goal: lightweight audience growth and traffic distribution.

Focus:
- useful AI/tool posts
- anti-slop point of view
- occasional funnel to Northory or TopGiftsToday

## Not now
- spreading effort across all domains
- trying to scale multiple YouTube channels at once
- building complex products before traffic and offers work

## Immediate next questions to answer
- What exact Northory offer should lead?
- What is the TopGiftsToday niche and first 30 article list?
- What content cadence is sustainable around the full-time job?
