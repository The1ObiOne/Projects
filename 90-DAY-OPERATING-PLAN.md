# 90-Day Operating Plan

## Objective
Create a realistic second-income system while keeping the full-time job stable.

## Business model
- Day job = stability
- Northory = high-margin side income
- TopGiftsToday = compounding affiliate asset
- WireRiot = audience and distribution

## Days 1 to 30
### Northory
- Finalize positioning and offer sheet
- Create one-page PDF or landing copy for the 3 offers
- Build a list of 30 to 50 target companies or contacts
- Start warm outreach
- Book discovery calls

### TopGiftsToday
- Set up site structure
- Join affiliate programs
- Publish first 10 to 12 articles
- Create Pinterest account and templates

### WireRiot
- Publish 1 to 2 useful posts per week
- Start shaping clear voice around useful AI and anti-slop positioning

## Days 31 to 60
### Northory
- Close 1 diagnostic
- Convert it into a sprint proposal
- Create sample artifact library from delivered work

### TopGiftsToday
- Reach 20 to 25 articles
- Interlink posts heavily
- Improve CTAs and product selection
- Start email capture

### WireRiot
- Continue lightweight posting
- Use posts to reinforce authority and point back to main properties

## Days 61 to 90
### Northory
- Deliver one sprint successfully
- Offer a small advisory retainer
- Document repeatable sales/process assets

### TopGiftsToday
- Reach 30 articles
- Double down on winners
- Publish seasonal content early
- Track affiliate clicks and commissions

### WireRiot
- Keep distribution steady, not overwhelming
- Promote the best-performing content and wins

## KPI targets
### Northory
- 30 to 50 prospects identified
- 5 to 10 strong outreach conversations
- 1 paid diagnostic
- 1 sprint closed

### TopGiftsToday
- 30 articles
- Pinterest workflow live
- early affiliate clicks
- first commissions if possible

### WireRiot
- consistent posting cadence
- basic audience growth
- traffic into owned assets

## Decision rules
- If Northory closes a meaningful client, protect delivery quality first
- If TopGiftsToday starts getting traction, expand only the winning content cluster
- If something needs daily attention, question whether it fits the life strategy

## Focus mantra
One company. One side-income engine. One passive-income bet. One distribution brand.
