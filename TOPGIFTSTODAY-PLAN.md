# TopGiftsToday Launch Plan

## Positioning
TopGiftsToday is a gift recommendation site for busy shoppers who want fast, trustworthy gift ideas.

## Starting niche
Start with a strong initial cluster, then expand outward.

Phase 1 cluster:
- men
- dads
- husbands
- boyfriends

Phase 2 expansion:
- women
- moms
- wives
- girlfriends
- friends
- coworkers

Layer on:
- birthdays
- anniversaries
- Father's Day
- Mother's Day
- Christmas
- Valentine's Day
- budgets like under $25, $50, and $100

## Monetization
