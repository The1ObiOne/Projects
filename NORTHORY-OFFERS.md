# Northory Offer Sheet

## Positioning
Northory helps small and mid-sized companies fix unreliable reporting, messy data pipelines, and leadership KPI chaos without hiring a full internal data team.

## Core offers

### 1. Data Stack Diagnostic
Best for teams that do not trust their dashboards, spend too much time pulling reports, or suspect pipeline/cost issues.

**Includes**
- Review of current data stack, pipelines, dashboards, and reporting workflows
- Identification of reliability, cost, and trust issues
- Prioritized roadmap
- 60-minute readout

**Deliverables**
- Findings memo
- Priority fix roadmap
- Executive summary

**Price**
- $2,500 to $5,000 fixed fee

**Timeline**
- 1 week elapsed time

### 2. Revenue or Ops Analytics Sprint
Best for one high-value outcome that can be shipped in 2 to 4 weeks.

**Examples**
- Build trustworthy executive KPI reporting
- Clean up dbt models and key pipelines
- Reduce warehouse cost
- Replace manual spreadsheet reporting
- Improve revenue or operations visibility

**Deliverables**
- One shipped analytics outcome
- Lightweight documentation
- Handoff notes

**Price**
- $7,500 to $20,000 fixed fee
- Sweet spot: $10,000 to $15,000

**Timeline**
- 2 to 4 weeks

### 3. Fractional Data Engineering Advisor
Best for teams that need senior guidance without a full-time hire.

**Includes**
- Async support by email/Slack
- Architecture and backlog review
- Vendor and tooling guidance
- One weekly call or two monthly calls

**Price**
- $1,500 to $4,000 per month

**Guardrails**
- Limited scope
- No full-time embedding
- Clear response expectations

## Ideal clients
- 10 to 100 employee SaaS, e-commerce, B2B services, or tech-enabled operations businesses
- Teams with data pain but no mature internal data leadership
- Buyers such as COO, VP Finance, Head of Ops, Product lead, or CTO

## Pain-led messaging
- Dashboards nobody trusts
- Manual CSV reporting every week
- Rising warehouse costs
- Board/finance reporting chaos
- No clean source of truth for metrics

## Sales sequence
1. Short intro call
2. Paid diagnostic
3. Fixed-scope sprint
4. Optional advisor retainer

## Outreach sentence
Northory helps operators fix unreliable reporting and messy data pipelines so leadership can trust the numbers again.
