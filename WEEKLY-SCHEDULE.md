# Weekly Schedule Around Full-Time Job

## Rule set
- Protect the day job first
- Use weeknights for focused build sessions only
- Use weekends for publishing, packaging, and batch work
- Avoid context-switching across too many brands

## Weekly cadence

### Monday, 60 to 90 minutes
- Northory outreach
- Follow up with leads
- Refine pitch or offer doc

### Tuesday, 60 to 90 minutes
- TopGiftsToday article drafting or editing
- Keyword and product research

### Wednesday, 60 to 90 minutes
- WireRiot content and distribution
- One useful post or thread
- Repurpose one insight from current work

### Thursday, 60 to 90 minutes
- TopGiftsToday publishing and Pinterest asset creation
- Affiliate link cleanup

### Friday, 30 to 60 minutes
- Review metrics
- Update TODOs
- Decide next week's priorities

### Saturday, 2 to 4 hours
- Deep work block
- Finish one Northory asset or publish one major TopGiftsToday article cluster

### Sunday, 2 to 3 hours
- Light planning
- Queue content
- Admin, cleanup, backups, and system improvements

## Time allocation target
- Northory: 3 to 5 hours/week
- TopGiftsToday: 4 to 6 hours/week
- WireRiot: 1 to 2 hours/week

## Do not do
- More than one active YouTube push at once
- More than one new business offer at once
- Endless branding tweaks instead of shipping
